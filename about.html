OVERVIEW

We're something other than duplicates… What's more, We do it right!

Dynamic Packaging Pvt. Ltd. is a leading provider of high-quality packaging solutions in South India. With over 25 years of experience in the industry, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for product manufacturers across various sectors.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility is equipped with advanced machinery, technology, and quality control measures that ensure the highest standards of product quality and safety. Our team of experts works closely with clients to understand their unique requirements and provide customised solutions that meet their specific needs.

OUR HISTORY

The evolution of Dynamic Packaging. A story of Perseverance and Growth

Dynamic Packaging was founded 25 years ago, in 1998, by B. Richard D'Souza in Tumkur District, Karnataka. Our company has a long and successful history in the print and packaging industries. From our humble beginnings, we have grown into a leading provider of printing and packaging solutions for businesses of all sizes.

Over the years, we have built a reputation for excellence in customer service, quality, and innovation. Through our commitment to excellence, we have earned the trust and loyalty of a growing number of customers across industries. Today, we continue to strive for excellence in everything we do, staying at the forefront of industry trends and technologies to provide our clients with the best possible service and solutions.

Today, we are proud to say that the second generation of the founder's family, i.e., G. Prashanth D'Souza and John P. D'Souza, is taking care of the business. Building on the strong foundation laid by the founder, the next generation is committed to continuing the company's legacy of excellence and innovation. With a deep understanding of the industry and a passion for delivering exceptional service and solutions, they are driving the company forward and positioning it for continued success in the years to come.
