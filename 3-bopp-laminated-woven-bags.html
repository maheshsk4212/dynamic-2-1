Overview

An Overview of
BOPP Laminated Woven Bags

BOPP (Biaxially oriented polypropylene) laminated woven bags combine the advantages of woven polypropylene bags and BOPP films. Widely used in agriculture, pet food, snacks, and retail products due to their high strength, durability, and profitability.

BOPP laminated woven bags are a versatile, strong, and reliable packaging solution with several benefits for businesses. Production involves extrudes, eliminators, and printing machines for consistent, high-quality production.
